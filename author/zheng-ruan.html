Motivation for the Blog

Mon 03 June 2013
By Zheng Ruan

In Motivation.

tags: motivationGoogle Summer of CodeGSoC

Originally, I plan to use google blogger system for GSoC blogging, as Peter Cock suggested. However, I learnt from Yanbo Ye this morning that github also support blogging system. I spent a day to play with it and finally understand the basic ways to set it up. pelican is different from CMS like wordpress, drupal and django as it focus on translate many types of mark language into html, thus faciliate blogging. I like this idea and hope I can have a wonderful coding summer.
